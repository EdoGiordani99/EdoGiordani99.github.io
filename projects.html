Apr 17 2022 NLP

Name Entity Recognition

Name Entity Recognition is an information extraction problem which consists in recognizing single word or group of words categories (such as persons, organizations, locations...) in an unstructured text. The model to be implemented will have to recognize 7 different categories. Except for the 'other' category, a prefix B is associated if the word marks the beginning of the name entity, or I if the word is found within it. In this report we will go through the whole design process for a NER deep learning model comparing a traditional solution approach with a novel one (possibly).
